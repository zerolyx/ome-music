# Ome Music 0.1.0

A calm desktop music player for your local library and NetEase Cloud Music, with a Bilibili video atmosphere and a natural DJ/podcast host.

## Quick Start

1. **Install** — Run `Ome Music_0.1.0_x64-setup.exe` and follow the NSIS installer. Windows 10/11 (x64) with WebView2 runtime.
2. **Import your music** — On first launch click **Choose Music Folder** on the welcome screen, then pick a folder of audio files (MP3, FLAC, M4A, OGG, WAV).
3. **Play** — Pick a track from the top search bar or let the room pick up cover light and play.

## Usage

- **Search** — Top-center bar searches your local library; enable NetEase / Bilibili in settings to search those sources too.
- **Transport** — Bottom bar: Shuffle and Loop (left), Play/Pause/Prev/Next (center), Volume/Mute (right). Drag the bottom slider to seek.
- **Lyrics** — Large synced lyrics fill the right side. Use the gear icon (top-right) → *Lyrics* to re-match, import a `.lrc`, or nudge timing by ±500ms.
- **Quick Settings** — Top-right gear: lyrics source, music source status, and playback quality (Hi-Res / Lossless / Extra High / Higher / Standard).
- **Ome Radio** — Left dock: pick a mood (Memory / Late / Soft / Drift) and *Start Broadcast* for a private, host-narrated session built from your listening taste.
- **DJ Curator** — Right dock: chat or speak a request ("play something calm", "a song like this one") and the host minds the records and talks between tracks.
- **Danmaku** — Bilibili tracks show light, pale floating comments over the video atmosphere. Tune density/motion in settings.

## Notes

- NetEase Cloud Music and Bilibili require their respective login in Settings before search/playback works.
- Credentials are stored in the OS keyring (not on disk).
